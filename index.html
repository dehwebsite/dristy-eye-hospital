    WELCOME TO DRISHTI EYE CARE HOSPITAL

    Run by the Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam Charitable Trust

    Drishti is a community eye care hospital - (A eye hospital for every one) – an eye hospital for all. It was founded in 2017 by a group of dedicated local people including skilled and experienced doctors.
    About Drishti Eye Care Hospital

    One Stop Solutions For all

    Eye Related Problems

    We treat all eye diseases. In addition, we tackle the major cause for reversible blindness in Bihar (cataract) with an ongoing programme that identifies the cataract-blind via village screening, transports patients to hospital and provides free surgery and aftercare. Since we started this work, around… people have had their sight restored and countless others prevented from going blind.

    From our inception, we have been supported by Mr. Ramesh KD Shah and SECOND SIGHT, UK, an organization founded by an ophthalmologist with hands-on experience working in rural Bihar. It is the only international NGO that has made Bihar its focus of attention. Their commitment is to support eye hospitals that put patients first, not profit.

    Drishti Eye Care Hospital Mission & Vision

    Mission:- To eradicate curable blindness among the rural Village by providing compassionate service, synergy with community, sustainable, quality curative and preventive eye care services.

    Vision:- Nobody with a curable condition remains blind in Aurangabad by the year 2025

    Our goals are to

    • Continuously improve all our services through quality management
    • Focus on our patients and deliver high-quality service
    • Involve all our partners in our quality improvement activities
    Super-Specialty Eye Care

    One Eye One Tree Initiative

    In June 2019, 30 people were brought to our hospital, who had unfortunately passed away due to heatstroke in a single day in Aurangabad, Bihar. We decided to involve the community and patients to understand the importance of tree plantation so that others would not lose their lives due to heatstroke.
